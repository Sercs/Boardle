Info & Tutorial

🔍 Discovery & Filters

  • Tap the ( ≡ ) Menu button at the top-right to filter routes by Grade, Angle, Holder, and more.
  • The Bottom Sheet tracks your search results:
    • Drag Handle up or down to adjust your browsing space.
    • Tap a climb to select and light it on the board.
    • Swipe Right on a climb entry to mark it as Sent.

🧗 Hold Interaction

  • Quick Tap: Instantly mark a hold for a general search.
  • Long Press (Hold): Open the Radial Scope.
    • Drag into a color-coded quadrant (e.g. Foot-only) and release to set a specific hold role.

🎮 Board Manipulation

  • Scope Mode: Tap and hold with 2 fingers to lock into panning. You can also release a finger to continue panning.
  • Pinch anywhere to Zoom. Zooming out significantly will Elastic Re-center the board to the middle.
  • Double Tap empty space to Quick Re-connect to the board if a session is established.
  • Flick Navigation: Swipe horizontally (Left/Right) on the board to cycle through your current climb list.

About Boardle

A companion app to the tension board 2 for seamless searching and sends!

✨ UI/UX Philosophy

  • Board Takes Center Stage: All controls are tucked away or overlayed only when needed, keeping your focus on the climbing surface.
  • Radial Accessibility: Every hold role is accessible with a single gesture and the same number of inputs—consistency is key.
  • Simultaneous Visibility: Browse the climb list without ever covering the board, allowing for instant recognition of routes.
  • Frictionless Logging: Record your sends with a single swipe, minimizing time spent in the app so you can get back to the wall.
